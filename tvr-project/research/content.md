- **H** - Helping
- **E** - Every
- **A** - American
- **R** - Reconnect
- **T** - Together
- Tagline: "Uniting Communities"
- Description: "Helping Every American Reconnect Together - uniting communities through service, compassion, and connection across California, Washington, and Pennsylvania."
- About: "The H.E.A.R.T. Initiative turns conversations from our podcast into action. Each host champions a local partner, coordinating real-world service that restores dignity and builds bridges. Together, we're building a nationwide movement rooted in unity and love."
