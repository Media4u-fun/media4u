## The Real Money Moves

### 1. Upgrades sell themselves
When a Starter client sees "Upgrade to unlock Booking" in their dashboard, some will upgrade. You don't have to sell them - the product does it.

### 2. Add-ons are pure profit
The modules are already built. Turning on mapping for a Starter client costs you nothing but makes you $49/month.

### 3. Setup fees cover your costs
The $500-$1,000 setup fee covers the time to deploy and configure their site. After that, monthly revenue is mostly profit since hosting costs are minimal.

### 4. Retention is king
A client paying $149/month for 3 years = $5,364 total revenue from one client. Focus on keeping clients happy over constantly finding new ones.

### 5. Scale without more work
Client #1 and client #100 use the same system. You don't need to hire developers as you grow. The factory does the heavy lifting.
